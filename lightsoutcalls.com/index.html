Started in a Garage in 2003

Wendell Rye stumbled across two flocks of wild turkeys near his home and the spark was ignited. What began with a few scratch box calls sold to friends has grown into a pursuit of the most natural sounding calls a hunter can carry.

After years of trial, prayer, and long hours at the lathe, three calls shipped to the Midwest Callmakers Competition in Wisconsin returned with two first-place awards. That was 2007. The calls have only gotten better since.
