The Process

From raw billet to finished edge, each knife goes through a rigorous process: heating, hammering, grinding, and hand finishing. We use traditional forge techniques combined with modern metallurgy to create blades that perform as well as they look.

  • Traditional fire welding and forge folding.
  • Modern steels including Damasteel and high-carbon alloys.
  • Hand-sharpened to a razor edge and polished by hand.
