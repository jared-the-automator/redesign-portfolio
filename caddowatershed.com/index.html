Our story

A Lifetime on the Water

Starr Bowen learned to canoe as a Boy Scout on the Brazos River. He built his first canoe in Scotland while studying theology, then spent years as a Methodist minister before dedicating himself full-time to the craft.

Since 2008, Caddo Watershed has built custom canoes and paddles for serious wilderness paddlers across the country. every boat is guaranteed to be treasured for a lifetime.
