About the store

Owned and run in Royse City.

Eagle Hardware Farm and Ranch is owned by Shawn Risinger. Family owned and operated, serving Hunt, Rockwall, and Kaufman counties with a wide range of hardware, farm supplies, tools, and livestock feed.

The staff has many years of experience and works hard to fill specific requests. Can't find what you need? Ask. If we carry it or can order it, we'll get it to you. We deliver to your home or barn.
