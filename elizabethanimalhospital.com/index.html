Our story

Owned by Dr. Leon Anderson since 1998

Dr. Anderson earned his veterinary degree from Kansas State University and runs the hospital with his wife, Trisha. He is a member of the AVMA, AHHA, and CVMA, and currently serves as a CVMA district representative.

Dr. Jen Redus, a Texas A&M graduate, joined the team in 2022, bringing shelter, emergency, and mobile practice experience to Elizabeth.
