Our Heritage

A Maine Tradition Since 1939

Maritime Energy started as a small family-run station serving local families in central Maine. Over eight decades, we have grown into one of the region's most trusted heating oil and propane providers.

Today, three generations of the family still work alongside our team of technicians, drivers, and customer service professionals. We are not just a fuel company. We are your neighbors.

Our delivery trucks, service vans, and certified technicians cover every corner of our community. When you choose Maritime Energy, you are choosing a family with ties to the local area that go back generations.
