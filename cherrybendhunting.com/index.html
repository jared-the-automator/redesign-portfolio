Our Story

Family, Tradition, Land

Cherrybend is family owned and operated by Matt and Beth Ellis and Scott and Melissa Ellis. The Ellis family has a long history farming on Cherrybend Road since the mid-1800's, and is dedicated to keeping the farm an Ohio pheasant hunting destination.

Our farm is fully operational and includes corn, soybeans, wheat, and the cover crops of sorghum and sunflowers. Beth Ellis serves as Manager for the Farm, with Ariane Sias handling daily operations and gamekeeping.
