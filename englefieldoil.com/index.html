Our Story

Founded in 1961 with humble beginnings

Our first office was in a basement where founder FW Englefield III and his wife managed the original three service stations. That same spirit of total dedication continues to drive every delivery today.

Today, Englefield Oil Company and our Duchess convenience stores are Greater Ohio's premier provider of automotive, commercial and industrial fuel and lubricant products.

With a statewide network, a fleet of dedicated service vehicles, four warehouse locations, and a commitment to safety, we invest in the people and equipment to exceed your expectations.
