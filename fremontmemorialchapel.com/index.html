Since 1987

A name Fremont has trusted for a generation

Francis G. Rose acquired the Lindsey chapel in 1987 and gave it the name Fremont families know today. The building on Peralta Boulevard has been a place of remembrance ever since, run by the same family, guided by the same belief that every life deserves a service made with care.
