Born on the Skeena

We field test every material we sell against wild steelhead and salmon in the Skeena drainage before it ever reaches a customer. That is the difference between a catalog and a fly supply house that actually fishes.

Located in Terrace, British Columbia, in the heart of the Skeena watershed, we are anglers first. If a material does not perform on our home water, we do not stock it.
