A Culver Road farmhouse, and 65 years of trust.

When Bob Dierna bought the old farmhouse on Culver Road in 1961, he built his funeral home on trust, service, and loving care. That tradition has never changed.

Today the home he started holds several chapels, a mortuary, a crematory, and three mausoleums set among rare international gardens. We treat every family as if they were our own.
