Our Story

Healing animals, protecting Bonaire.

Bonaire Veterinary Center was founded by a team of veterinarians who believe every animal deserves expert care, regardless of species or circumstance. From the family dog to the stray cat on the beach, we are here to help.

Our clinic is equipped with modern surgical and diagnostic tools, and our team works closely with local animal welfare organizations to reduce stray populations and improve community health.
