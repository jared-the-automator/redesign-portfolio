Built By Hand in New Hampshire

Every Newfound kit starts as a stack of cedar in our New Hampshire workshop. Cedar strips are milled to exact dimensions, fiberglass and epoxy are measured and packed, and full-size plans are rolled and shipped to your door. You supply the garage, the weekends, and the patience. We supply everything else.

Rose Woodyard, co-owner, works alongside the crew to make sure each kit arrives complete.
