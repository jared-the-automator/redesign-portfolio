Horses, too

A clinic that makes house calls to the barn

Dr. Rebecca Vollrath Severn began equine work in 2016, with experience in sports medicine on an elite hunter-jumper circuit and externships at equine hospitals in Florida and Wisconsin. Today she serves horses within a 40-mile radius of Houghton, with monthly trips to Marquette.

That means wellness exams, vaccinations, dentistry, lameness diagnosis, emergency care, and reproductive services, including artificial insemination, all brought to your property.

Our clinic

A new facility, built on more than twenty years of care

Dr. Rebecca Vollrath Severn started at Copper Country Veterinary Clinic as a high-school assistant and purchased the practice in 2019. The team moved into a brand-new building three miles outside Houghton in June of 2021, with room for kennels, treatment, surgery, and equine work under one roof.
