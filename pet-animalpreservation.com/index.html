A Missouri studio built on patience.

Anthony Eddy learned taxidermy the slow way, by hand, on the animals of hunters and families across the state. The studio treats every piece as someone's memory, not a product.

That same care now extends to family pets, with freeze-dry preservation done quietly and respectfully.
