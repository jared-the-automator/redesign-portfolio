What we offer

Programs built around time in the saddle

Riding lessons

Ground work and mounted instruction for beginners through experienced riders, matched to each rider's pace.

02

Summer riding camps

Week-long camps where kids learn horse care, riding, and the steady confidence that comes from working with animals.

03

Cross-country course and trails

A full course with logs, banks, and a water complex, plus wooded trails for schooling and relaxed rides.

Beyond the farm

The Mobile Classroom brings horses to you

The Equine Discovery Center's Mobile Classroom travels to schools, libraries, and community groups across the region. Students meet a horse up close and learn about care, behavior, and the world of these animals without leaving their own town.

It is the same hands-on approach that defines the farm, packed into a visit that fits a classroom or a fairground.
