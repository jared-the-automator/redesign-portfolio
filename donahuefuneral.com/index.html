Our story

Four generations, one family.

Great grandfather James P. Donahue brought the family into the funeral business before directors were formally licensed. His grandson entered the trade in the early 1900s across Philadelphia, and today Philip A. Donahue carries the fourth generation, serving families throughout Bucks County and beyond.

For those who served

Veteran benefits, handled with care.

We arrange veteran burial benefits at no additional cost to the family. That includes a gravesite in any of the 131 national cemeteries with available space, opening and closing of the grave, perpetual care, a government headstone or marker, a burial flag, and a Presidential Memorial Certificate. Spouses and dependents are honored alongside the veteran.
