What We Do

Three ways to walk with llamas

01

Day hikes, overnight trips, and custom pack trips in Capitol Reef, Utah and Jackson Hole, Wyoming.

02

Llama support for backcountry hunters. Quiet, steady animals that stay with your string and haul the gear.

03

Rentals, breeding, clinics, and llama rendezvous. Learn to pack your own string under Beau and Kirstin's guidance.
