A Real Wilderness
Camp Experience

Our camp is located 55 miles northeast of Jackson, Wyoming. Hot breakfast, sack lunch, and hot dinner. Canvas wall tents, wood stoves, and a full-time cook. This is not a luxury lodge. This is the real thing.

  • 12-14 canvas wall tents with wood stoves
  • Full-time cook serving three hot meals daily
  • Hot shower tent and generator power
  • Calm, well-broke horses with all tack supplied
