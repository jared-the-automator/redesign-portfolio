Over Two Decades of Craft

For over 20 years, Homer Decoys has been making decoys the old-fashioned way. Based in Conowingo, Maryland, we manufacture our foam decoys and decoy heads using all recycled materials right here in the United States.

Originally founded in Piqua, Ohio, the company was sold to the Hash family and relocated to Maryland. Today we carry on the tradition of quality foam decoys that hunters trust season after season.
