Heritage

Circumnavigations. Records. And a half-century of trust.

Apart from class dinghys, there are probably more Hartley Boats throughout the world than any other design.

Our boats have competed in races from club sailing in Hong Kong and England to the Cape to Rio and Sydney Hobart.

Workboats have been accepted by governments from the Singapore Harbour Authority to the Danish Fisheries College. Some were used for commercial crayfishing, conventional fishing, and abalone diving.
