Our Story

Born on the Banks of the Madison

Montana Angling Company was built by people who would rather be on the water than anywhere else. Owner and guide Max Yzaguirre started this company because he believed fly fishing in Montana should be led by locals who know the river the way a rancher knows his land.

Today, that same principle guides every trip we run. We do not chase crowds. We chase the right drift, the right hatch, and the right moment on Montana water.

A Montana Outfitters & Guides Association member and featured in Trout Unlimited.
