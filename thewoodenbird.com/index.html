The Carver

One block of tupelo. One ribbon at a time.

Rick Mignano has been awarded over 50 ribbons from competitions across Louisiana, Wisconsin, Ohio, Ward World, and online carved decoy contests. Each bird begins as a rough form of tupelo, shaped by hand until it breathes.
