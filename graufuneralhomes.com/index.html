Our Story

A Family Tradition

Grau Funeral Homes has served families across Northeast Iowa with dignity, respect, and unwavering support for generations. What began as a single funeral home has grown into a network of eight locations, each deeply rooted in its community.

Our team of dedicated funeral directors and staff understands that every life is unique, and every family deserves personalized care. We are here to listen, guide, and help you create a meaningful tribute.
