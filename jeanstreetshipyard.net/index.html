Two Years Before
Florida Was a State

Jean Street Shipyard opened in 1843, still at the same address on the Hillsborough River. That makes it Florida's oldest shipyard, and one of the longest-running boatyards in the South.

It started as a hardware supplier for local boatbuilders. Over 183 years it grew into a full repair, refit, and restoration yard with a wood shop, metal fabrication shop, and a ship's store that still carries the marine hardware Tampa Bay boaters have relied on for generations.

"We have been in the same place, doing the same thing since 1843."
