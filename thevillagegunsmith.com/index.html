Our Story

Built by Hand, One Part at a Time

In 1984, Kenneth Pacheco began a project to show the type of work he could do. Starting with a 2 1/4 inch cherry plank, he fabricated every small part by hand as a gunsmith would have done in the late 1700s. The rifle took 852 hours to build and spanned four years to complete.

That same dedication defines every repair, restoration, and custom build that comes through the door. From vintage Colt and Smith & Wesson revolvers to modern custom target pistols, Ken treats every firearm with the same precision and care that went into that flintlock rifle.
