Shooting Community

Rio Salado Sportsman's Club sits on 40 acres of Arizona desert just east of Mesa. From first-time shooters to competitive competitors, the facilities serve anyone who wants to shoot in a safe, well-maintained environment.

The club hosts events throughout the year for members and guests, with a full board of directors governing operations and ensuring the range stays accessible for generations of shooters.
