Guest Ranch

Experience the 7 Lazy P Guest Ranch

Rooted in Montana tradition since 1931, our guest ranch is a perfect blend of history, rustic comfort, and hospitality. Whether you are looking for a traditional wilderness pack trip, a relaxing guest ranch getaway, or a fully guided outdoor adventure, our experienced team is dedicated to helping guests experience Montana's backcountry in an authentic and memorable way.

Guests from across the country visit the 7 Lazy P to experience horseback travel, wilderness camping, fly fishing, hiking, hunting, wildlife viewing, and the unmatched scenery of the Bob Marshall Wilderness.
