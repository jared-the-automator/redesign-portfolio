Our family

Founded by Bob and Joan Winfield. Still run by the family.

The Anderson-Winfield Funeral Home was founded in 1987 by Robert and Joan Winfield, along with Charlene Winfield Capuano and Thomas Winfield. It carries on the Winfield Funeral Home, a name serving Rhode Island families since 1928.

Today Charlene or Tom will guide you through every arrangement, and see that nothing is left for you to worry about. The knowledge, the experience, and the patience are all still here.
