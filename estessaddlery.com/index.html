A Decade of
Learning the Craft

Ted has always had an appreciation for a comfortable, working saddle since childhood. After learning the art of proper repair by restoring innumerable saddles, he began the pursuit of saddle making.

In 2006, Ted applied for and received a TCAA (Traditional Cowboy Arts Association) scholarship and spent a week with Steve Mecum in Crowheart, Wyoming. A vast amount of knowledge was obtained and still appreciated and utilized in the shop today.
