Our Story

It Began Back
In High School

Smokey River Bows started as a high school dream that grew into a lifelong craft. Every bow is still shaped one at a time, by hand, in a small Kansas workshop. The camo survival recurve, the towering English war bow, the first longbow for a young shooter, each one carries the same attention to grain, balance, and quiet pride.

We do not run a factory. We make bows. And every bow leaves the shop ready to earn its stories in the woods.
