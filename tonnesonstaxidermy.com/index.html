Three Generations in the Field

Dave's School of Taxidermy was built by hunters, for hunters. What started as a passion for preserving memories has grown into a trusted North Dakota institution serving sportsmen across the region.

We believe taxidermy is not just a trade. It is an art form that honors the animal, the hunt, and the tradition of conservation. Our students leave with more than a certificate. They carry the skills to build a livelihood.
