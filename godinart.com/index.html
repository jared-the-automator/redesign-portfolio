A Lifetime of Wildfowl

Pat Godin is a 14-time World Champion wildfowl artist. A master carver with a passion for wildfowl and nature, his work has been recognized at the highest levels of the competitive carving world.

Each decoy begins with careful observation. Pat studies the posture, plumage, and character of each species before he picks up his knife. The result is a piece of art that captures the essence of a living bird.

His work spans decorative decoys, gunning decoys, lifesize wildfowl sculptures, and miniature pieces. Each is a signed original.
