Crystal River and Homosassa: The World's Best Kept Secret

Crystal River is home to the world's largest population of the endangered West Indian Manatee. It is also the only destination in the United States that permits swimming with these gentle giants. Whether casting a line or swimming with manatees, the Nature Coast delivers.
