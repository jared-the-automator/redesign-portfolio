The Captain

David Beede knows these waters like his own backyard.

David started Shallow Point Charters in 1988, long before Tampa Bay became the fishing destination it is today. He grew up poling the same flats, reading the same mangrove lines and watching the same bait schools that he still chases with clients today.

Whether you are after your first redfish or a personal best snook, David tailors each trip to the conditions, the season and your experience level. All gear is provided, tackle is top-tier, and the goal is always the same: fish.
