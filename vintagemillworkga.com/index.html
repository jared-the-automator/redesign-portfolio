The pocket door frame that built this company.

Vintage Millwork started in 1974 with two products: dentil mouldings and pocket door frames. Both are still made here, to the same standard, by the same family operation.

Builders and architects across Georgia have specified our pocket door frames for decades. Frames ship to projects daily across the United States.

Our facility sits near the intersection of I-20 and I-285 in Atlanta. Custom orders are quoted same-day and ready within days, not weeks.
