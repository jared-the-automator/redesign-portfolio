Our Story

Five Generations of Keeping Maine Warm

M.W. Sewall has been a fixture in Midcoast Maine since 1887. What started as a small fuel operation in Bath has grown into a full-service energy company, but we have never lost the personal touch that comes from being family-owned.

We are more than fuel delivery. We provide full-service heating installations, repairs, and tune-ups to meet all your home and business needs. Our technicians live here, raise families here, and understand what a Maine winter demands.

When you call M.W. Sewall, you talk to someone who knows your neighborhood. That is the difference 139 years makes.
