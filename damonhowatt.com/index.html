The Maker

Built by Hand. Proven in the Field.

Every Damon Howatt bow starts with seasoned hardwood and ends with a shooter who knows the difference between a product and a legacy. These are not factory bows. They are built one at a time, by people who still believe a bow should feel alive in your hand.

01

Seasoned Wood, Zero Compromise

Only the finest seasoned hardwoods touch a Howatt bow. Each piece is selected for grain, density, and resilience before it ever reaches the bench.

02

100% USA Crafted

From Oregon timber to finished limb, every step of the build happens under one roof in Lebanon. No outsourcing, no shortcuts.

03

Backed by 85 Years

Since 1939, the name Damon Howatt has has been synonymous with the finest traditional bows in America. One bow at a time, one shooter at a time.

The Craft

One Bow. One Craftsman. One Purpose.

In a world of factory lines, we still build one bow at a time. Each Damon Howatt bow is shaped, tillered, and finished by hand. The result is not a SKU; it is a tool that becomes an extension of the archer who draws it.

Our workshop in Lebanon, Oregon, is not a showroom. It is a working space where sawdust covers the floor and the smell of linseed oil greets you at the door. This is where tradition lives.
