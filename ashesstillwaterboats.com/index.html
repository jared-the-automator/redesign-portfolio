A Story Begins

From Fragments of the Past, a New Narrative

"Ashes started with an idea; that fragments from one's past could be crafted into something unique, not at all like the original but evoking it in its complete essence."

Designer Trevor Paetkau, a graduate of the Creative Writing Program at UBC, has been spinning tales for decades. Now he channels that storytelling into every line of every plan. When you build an Ashes canoe, you are building more than a boat. You are building a chapter of your own story.
