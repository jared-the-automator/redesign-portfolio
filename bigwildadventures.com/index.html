Year 48

Big Wild Adventures was founded in 1978 by Howie Wolke. We are a family business, owned by Jesse Ford and David Ellis. All trips have the personal touch of experienced owners, and we are happy to accommodate student groups and folks with disabilities.

We have run well over one thousand multi-day wilderness treks across the American West and Alaska. As featured in Backpacker, Sierra, National Geographic Adventure, Outside, The New York Times, Outside Bozeman and more.
