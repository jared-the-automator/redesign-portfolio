The Maker

Forty-Five Years at the Bench.

Doug grew up on an Idaho ranch, hiring out for day work across the West. In 1976, he started training under master saddlemakers Bob Kelly and Ray Holes. By 1981, he had his own shop in Montana. He relocated to Nevada in 2000, where he still works every piece by hand.

His saddles have been featured at the Santa Ynez Historical Museum and the Western Folklife Center, and are held in private collections worldwide. He still never builds the same saddle twice.
