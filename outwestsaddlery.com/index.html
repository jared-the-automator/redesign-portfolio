The Work

Old-time construction.
Built for today's horses.

01

Wade and Vaquero trees

All saddles built on Wade-style trees with wide bars. Herman Oak leather, rough out or smooth side out, oiled and treated with Skidmore's Leather Cream. Saddle fully covered, all stitching recessed.

02

Every saddle is numbered

Over 100 saddles built and cataloged, each with the owner's initials and serial number. No two alike. Average weight is 32 pounds. Base price $5,600.

03

Fitted to your horse

Lisa Rensch combines saddle fitting with whole-horse health evaluation. The fit is the starting point, not an afterthought. She works from photos and direct assessment before any build begins.

Built in Salmon, Idaho.
Shipped to Australia.

Out West Saddlery was founded by Bob Beecher, who built over 100 custom saddles from his shop in Salmon, Idaho. In January 2026, Lisa Rensch took over the shop, continuing the same build philosophy: old-time construction, therapeutic fit, no shortcuts.

Saddle #70 was built on a Slick 9 tree for the film Out Of The Wild, based on the novel by horseman and clinician Mark Rashid. Customers have come from Texas, Australia, and across the mountain West.
