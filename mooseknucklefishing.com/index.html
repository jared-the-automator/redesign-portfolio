The crew

A handful of anglers, one bad habit.

Chris, Brenton, Zach, Dave, George, and Zombo have been chasing fish together for more than a decade. MKFF is the excuse to keep doing it, and the place to tell the stories that come back with us.

From Sanibel snook to Catskill cane country, the plan is simple: fish hard, tie your own, and don't leave anyone on the bank.
