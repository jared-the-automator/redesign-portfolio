The Craftsman

A carpenter's hands. An angler's eye.

Rocky started building rods in the late 70s as a hobby for himself and friends. Word spread. Before long, anglers from across New Jersey were asking for rods wrapped in his signature snakeskin.

A retired carpenter and Vietnam War veteran, Rocky brings the same precision he once applied to timber and trim to every rod he builds. Over fifty years on the water. Over three decades at the bench.

He prefers freshwater fishing, chasing trout, walleye, muskie, and bass on Lake Hopatcong and the local streams. He makes every type of rod, and he uses every type of rod he makes.
