Oil facts

What today's oil heat actually delivers

Cleaner than the reputation

Today's ultra-low-sulfur heating oil burns at near-zero particulate emissions. A modern oil furnace is cleaner than a wood stove, cleaner than the diesel in the truck that delivers it.

More heat per gallon

Heating oil produces 138,500 BTU per gallon. Propane delivers 91,500. You get 50% more heat from every gallon that goes into your tank.

Your fuel, on your property

No pipeline dependency. No utility monopoly. Your heat is stored in a tank you own, delivered by a company you chose.

Equipment that lasts

Oil-fired boilers and furnaces routinely run for 25 to 30 years with basic annual maintenance. Gas equipment averages 12 to 15.
