The Maker

From the GM Lab
to the Workshop Bench

Ziggie Obidzinski spent 40 years at General Motors as a Research and Development Technician, Designer, and Manager. Metals, plastics, tolerances, manufacturing quality. He retired in 1999 and brought those disciplines to a workshop in Marine City, a few miles from where his grandfather first put a fishing rod in his hands.

He spent his early retirement competing in ice decoy carving. Multiple wins at the World and National Fish Decoy Championships. Then his Muskie fishing friends started asking him to repaint their lures.

In 2003, he made 150 lures and passed them out to his fishing circle on the St. Clair. They pulled some very large muskies that year.

Now the word is out.
