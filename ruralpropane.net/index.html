Our Story

Serving the Tobacco Valley Since 2014

Rural Propane Services, LLC began with a simple goal: make sure no family in Northwest Montana goes cold in winter. As a wholly-owned subsidiary of Lincoln Electric Cooperative, we bring the resources of a larger organization with the responsiveness of a local team.

Our drivers live in the communities they serve. When the thermometer drops below zero and your gauge is running low, you need someone who knows the back roads and can get there fast.
