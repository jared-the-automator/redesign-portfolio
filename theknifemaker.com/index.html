FORGED STEEL / DAMASCUS

A blade starts as a piece of steel and ends as a tool that outlasts its owner. Dan Graves forges, grinds, heat-treats, and handles every piece himself in a small shop with a coal forge and an anvil. There are no shortcuts, no production lines, and no compromises on steel quality.

Sourcing only the highest grades of carbon and damascus steel, each knife is built to perform in the field and last for generations.
