Our Story

Born and Raised in the Maine Woods

Shepard Hunting Company has been guiding hunters through the Stacyville woods for over a decade. We are not a booking agency — we live here, we hunt here, and we know the land because it is where we grew up.

Ryan Shepard and his team of registered Maine guides bring the kind of local knowledge that only comes from decades spent in the field. Our hunts are small, personal, and built around your goals — whether that is a bear over bait or a moose in Zone 6.
