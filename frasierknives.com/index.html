Since 2013, One Maker, One Forge

In the rugged landscape of NW Montana, a blade is more than steel. It is a tool shaped by hand, heat, and purpose. Every knife begins as raw stock, is heated to forging temperature, and is worked with deliberate, practiced strokes until the form matches the function.

No two knives leave the forge identical. Each is built for a specific hand, a specific task, a specific life. That is the promise of a custom knife: not perfect symmetry, but perfect fit.
