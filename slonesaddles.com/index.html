The Slone Story

A family tradition since the first rodeo season.

For more than 40 years, the Slone family has hand-built custom saddles in the heart of Texas. What started as a small workshop in Cuero has grown into a trusted name on the national stage — not by changing the standard, but by refusing to lower it.

Whether outfitting a champion or awarding a trophy, the workmanship never changes. That is what four decades in the rodeo industry looks like.
