01 / The Craft

Every Bow Tells a Story

We do not manufacture bows. We reveal them from carefully selected wood. Each model draws on centuries of archery tradition, from English longbows to Assyrian horse bows.

01

Select Materials

Only the finest woods and laminates touch our workshop. No shortcuts, no fillers.

02

Hand Shaping

Each bow is worked by hand, tillered to precise draw weight, and tested for performance.

03

Finish & Seal

Natural oils and protective finishes that age with the bow, not against it.

02 / The Bowyer

A Dream Since Childhood

The wooden bow has been my dream since I can remember. This dream turned into a passion that continues to this day.

In 2018, I founded the official Will Bowmaker workshop. Each bow is created by hand from the highest quality materials. Some are dedicated for historical reenactors. Others, such as fantasy bows, are for a wider circle of archery enthusiasts.

What unites them is the desire to create a well-shooting bow.

Founded in 2018, making bows since 2007.
