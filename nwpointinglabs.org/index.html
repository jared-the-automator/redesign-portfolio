Our mission

Keeping the pointing instinct alive in the Northwest.

The club exists to preserve and promote naturally pointing Labrador retrievers across the Pacific Northwest. We share breeder links, trainer links, and training tips so the next dog is better than the last.

Members get the club directory, hunt test entries, and first look at titled dogs in the region.
