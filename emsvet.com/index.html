Our story

Since 1999, the same standard.

Equine Medical Service was established in 1999 in Laporte, Colorado. The practice was built on a simple measure: treat the horse in front of you with the same care you would want for your own. That standard has not changed across two and a half decades of Northern Colorado practice.

The team handles the everyday and the difficult alike, from routine wellness to the cases other clinics refer out.
