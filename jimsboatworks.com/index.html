Restoration

Bring the Old Back to Life

Wood canvas canoes and square-stern boats absorb the history of every lake, river, and marsh they have known. When the canvas frays and the ribs weaken, it does not mean the end. It means the beginning of another chapter.

Our restoration process rebuilds from the keel up, replacing worn ribs with matched white cedar, stretching new canvas, and finishing with the same hand-rubbed oil that made the original so beautiful.
