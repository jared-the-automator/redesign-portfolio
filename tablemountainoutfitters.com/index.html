Our Story

Built by Hunters,
for Hunters

Table Mountain Outfitters was founded on one simple belief: that a great hunt starts with great preparation and even better country. Based in Wyoming, we have spent years building relationships with landowners, studying migration patterns, and learning the terrain that produces trophy animals.

From the first scouting trip to the final pack-out, we handle the logistics so you can focus on the hunt. Our camp is comfortable, our guides are skilled, and our commitment to your success is absolute.
