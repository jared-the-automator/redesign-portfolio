Ken Weymouth builds bamboo fly rods in a Wisconsin shop. Each one is hand-planed from Tonkin cane, inscribed, and fished hard for generations.

Browse the Collection Contact Ken

Every rod begins as a six-strip hexagon of Tonkin cane.

01

Hand planed and heat-tempered to taper before gluing. Ken's rod tapers are his own: fifteen years of trial on Wisconsin trout water, refined one casting stroke at a time.

02

Mortised nickel silver reel seats, custom-turned flor cork grips, silk thread wraps, and spar varnish finish. Every component chosen for decades of hard fishing, not a production floor.

03

Each rod leaves inscribed with its specs and serial number, shipped in a WRW-monogrammed rod bag and brass-capped aluminum tube. Signed. Documented. Ready to fish.

Signature trout rods from $1,500. Steelhead and spey from $2,100. Custom builds available.
