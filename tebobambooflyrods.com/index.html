The Maker

Each Rod is
Personal

Summer is peak fly fishing season. The rivers are running clear and the hatches are thick. A bamboo rod has the rhythm and feel that graphite cannot match, turning every cast into a conversation with the water.

Robert Thibeau builds each rod by hand in his workshop, selecting every strip of bamboo with care. The result is a rod with a soul, not just a tool.
