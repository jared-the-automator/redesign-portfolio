The Craft

Fifteen years of American made furniture

Mountain Top Furniture has built solid wood heirlooms in Blairsville, Georgia for more than fifteen years. Every table starts as a raw slab and leaves as a piece meant to outlast the house it sits in.

The work is local, the wood is honest, and the finish is meant to age with the family that owns it.
