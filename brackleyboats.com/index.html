30 Years in Muskoka

The last boat-
building tradition
worth preserving.

Gravenhurst was the boat-building capital of Canada. Minett, Greavette, Duke, Ditchburn — the great makers who put mahogany runabouts on every lake in cottage country. Paul Brackley learned the trade in that tradition and has kept it alive.

Clients bring us boats that have been in families for generations. Boats with stories. We don't take the responsibility of that lightly.
