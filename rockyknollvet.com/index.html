Two veterinarians, one family practice

Built on 25 years of barn calls

Dr. Marylou Rings founded Rocky Knoll in 2001 after graduating from The Ohio State University College of Veterinary Medicine. She grew up on a cotton farm in West Texas and has spent her career caring for livestock across central Ohio.

Dr. Mike Rings joined the practice in 2012. He is a Diplomate of the American College of Veterinary Internal Medicine and spent 37 years as a professor of veterinary clinical sciences before bringing that depth to the field.

Marylou Brown Rings, DVM

Founder, 2001

A graduate of The Ohio State University College of Veterinary Medicine, Dr. Rings grew up on a West Texas cotton farm and built Rocky Knoll from the ground up. When she is not on a farm call, she rides her American Quarter Horse, Jake, and shows her Irish Setter, Rory.

Mike Rings, DVM

Diplomate, ACVIM (Large Animal)

Dr. Rings holds an MS from the University of Minnesota and is a Diplomate of the American College of Veterinary Internal Medicine. He taught veterinary clinical sciences for 37 years, winning numerous teaching awards, before joining the practice in 2012.
