Our Story

Built on Respect for the Hunt

Steve Mudgett has been an animal enthusiast since childhood, surrounded by hunting and farming in Massachusetts. He began learning taxidermy under mentor Bob Watkins of Millbury, refining a craft that honors both the animal and the hunter.

Deershorn serves all of New England, catering to sportsmen who expect high quality and lifelike work that preserves the memory of every hunt.
