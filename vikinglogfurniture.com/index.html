A Minnesota Manufacturer That Will Never Be a Factory

In 1979, we started building log bedroom furniture in a small shop outside St. Joseph. Today that same workshop manufactures 14 bed styles, dining sets, pool tables, and kitchen cabinets. Every piece still ships from the same address.

We hand-select every log, control every step from raw pine to finished piece, and back the result with a lifetime warranty because we stand behind what we build. Custom dimensions and configurations are always welcome.

"We will never become a factory for mass production."

Mike Legatt, President
