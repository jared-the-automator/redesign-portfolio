The Central Flyway

A Story Worth Leading With

The Central Flyway has long been known as one of the most productive waterfowl corridors in North America. From the prairie potholes to the river bottoms, the migration routes that pass through this region have drawn hunters for generations.

Dakota Decoy Company was built on that heritage. Every product we make is tested in the same marshes and fields where our customers hunt. If it does not hold up here, it does not leave the shop.
