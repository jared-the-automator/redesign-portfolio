Our Story

Built on Lake Champlain Time

Snake Mountain Boatworks began with a 1938 Chris Craft and a belief that old boats deserve better than a barn. That hull is still on the water today.

Owner Michael Claudon brings decades of experience to every project, from engine rebuilds to planking and paint. If it floats and it is wood, we speak its language.
