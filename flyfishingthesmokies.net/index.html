The waters we fish

One region, a lot of water. Trips move with the season, the flows, and what is fishing best the week you come.

01

Great Smoky Mountains streams

Freestone park water for wild brook, brown, and rainbow trout. Classic small-stream fishing under the rhododendron.

02

Tuckasegee River

A year-round tailwater with room to wade or drift. Steady trout numbers make it a strong day for newer anglers.

03

Little Tennessee River

Big-water float days for smallmouth bass, walleye, and trout when the season lines up.

04

Cherokee, North Carolina

Guided trophy trout water near Cherokee, a good call when you want a real shot at a large fish.

05

Backcountry brook trout

Hike-in native brookie streams for anglers who will trade easy access for wild, untouched water.

06

Carp on the fly

A patient, technical hunt for anglers who have caught plenty of trout and want a new problem to solve.
