A New England fuel name since 1930

Volta Oil has fueled homes and businesses across New England since 1930, from its base in Plymouth, Massachusetts. For nearly a century, customers across the region have counted on the same Volta Oil truck in the driveway when the cold sets in.

That trust is built one delivery at a time, and it shows up in every part of the company, from heating oil routes to the Rapid Refill stores on the corner.
