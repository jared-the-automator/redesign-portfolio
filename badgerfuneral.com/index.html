Our History

Founded in Groton, Massachusetts in 1921 by Harris L. Badger, our funeral home has remained a pillar of this community for over a century. Four generations later, we still answer the phone personally, serve families face-to-face, and treat every service as if it were our own family.

Today, Badger Funeral Home proudly operates locations in both Littleton and Groton. Our dedicated team of licensed directors and caring staff continues the tradition of gentle, personalized service that has defined our family for one hundred years.
