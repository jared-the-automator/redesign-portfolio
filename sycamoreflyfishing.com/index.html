About Preston

A patient guide with deep local knowledge

Preston has spent years on the rivers and streams of Tennessee, Virginia, and North Carolina. He knows where the fish sit when the water drops, where to cast when the cicadas are loud, and how to make a first-timer feel at home on a fly rod.

Sycamore Fly Fishing is built on one idea: make the sport enjoyable and approachable for everyone. If you want to learn, you will. If you want to catch your personal best, that happens too.
