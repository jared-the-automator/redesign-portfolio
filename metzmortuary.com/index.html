Our history

Since 1891

Metz Mortuary grew out of the furniture and undertaking business that Charles Baer and Burnard King started in York in 1891. A.A. Metz came to work for the firm in 1903 and purchased it the following year.

Four generations of local families have owned and run the mortuary since. Jon Brouillette leads it today, from the Lincoln Avenue building the firm moved into in 1978.
