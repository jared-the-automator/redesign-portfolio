The Longbow Story

Every Bow Has a History

A couple years back, a customer gifted me an older Sunset Hill string follow longbow. At 53 pounds, it was too heavy to shoot comfortably. I reached out to bowyer Nate Steen.

He took it in for its fourth and final rework. Back came a 65.5 inch nock-to-nock, 43 pound beauty renamed Saucon, complete with creamy plum brown glass back, a narrow teardrop grip, and limbs deeper and narrower than a regular Hill.

That is the kind of connection we build with our gear and our customers. Good equipment deserves respect.
