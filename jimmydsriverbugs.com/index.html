Meet Jimmy D

A passionate angler from Sartell, Minnesota, Jimmy D designed an array of custom hand-tied hair jigs and flies that are proven to catch fish. From river smallmouth to walleye on the lakes, his creations helped anglers put more fish in the boat.

"At Jimmy D's River Bugs my aim is for you to catch fish and to be a satisfied customer. If you like catching Small Mouth Bass in moving water, you simply have to try my River Bugs. They have taken tens of thousands of fish with no end in sight."
