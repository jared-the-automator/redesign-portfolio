What David Does

01

Custom Stocks

Built from blank to fitted finish. Duplication, full custom, or a fit adjustment to the gun already in your hands.

02

Checkering

Hand-cut at 18 to 28 LPI. Ribbons, stippling, flame patterns, traditional point, or combinations. The kind of work people notice across the rack.

03

Restoration

Crack repair, re-checkering, oil and gloss refinishing. Bringing guns back to the condition they deserve, and the condition they were meant to be in.

Started at
the Gun Club

David Campbell spent his career in construction, but his evenings on a clay range near Atlanta. When he started refinishing and checkering his own guns, other shooters at the club started asking who did the work.

Today he works on the guns that matter most to their owners: Perazzi, Caesar Guerini, Perugini, Browning, L.C. Smith, Remington. Federal Firearms Licensee. Appointments at the shop.

"Stop by his shop if you ever get the chance. You will be shocked at what he can do with wood." Tim Owens, shooting instructor
