Our Story

Founded in 1958.
Still Family.

Rayfield and Ruby Richardson started R.N.T. Funeral Home in Warner Robins, Georgia in 1958. Their belief was straightforward: every family deserves to say goodbye with dignity, regardless of means.

Nearly 70 years later, Richardson Funeral Homes serves families across Perry and Warner Robins under the same name and the same principle. Two locations. Still family-owned.

When you call Richardson, you reach someone who has served your neighbors, and very likely your family before.
