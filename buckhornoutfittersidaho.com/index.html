The hunts

Buck Horn Outfitters runs fair-chase guided hunts across the units around Yellow Pine. Every trip is built around the animal you came for and the ground your guide knows best.

01

Elk and mule deer

Archery and rifle seasons in Idaho's backcountry, with pack support into the high country.

02

Black bear and predator

Spring and fall bear hunts, plus predator calling trips built around the late-season push.

03

Mountain lion and wolf

Hound-driven lion hunts and authorized wolf harvest, guided end to end.

Guided trout fly fishing

The same drainages that hold elk hold native trout. Buck Horn Outfitters guides walk-and-wade and float trips on the local rivers, timed to the hatches.

  • - Half and full day walk-and-wade trips
  • - Float trips on the surrounding rivers
  • - Gear and flies provided, all skill levels welcome

Backcountry on horseback
