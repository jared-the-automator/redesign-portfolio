"For over 25 years, I've built relationships with hunters from around the world. Whether your trophy was taken in Montana or Africa, Cat Tracks Wildlife Design will capture the most lifelike representation."

The studio is in Broadus, at the edge of the Powder River country that serious big-game hunters travel thousands of miles to reach. Paul lives there. He guides in the fall, mounts through the winter, and puts the same attention into every piece that leaves the shop.
