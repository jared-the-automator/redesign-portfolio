Our Story

Four decades of care in
one Harrison County clinic

For over 40 years, Smith Animal Clinic has been the place where New Salisbury families bring their pets when it matters most. We have grown up alongside the community, and many of our clients have been coming to us since their first childhood pet.

That continuity matters. When you walk through our doors, you are not a number on a chart. You are a neighbor, and your pet is family. Our team dedicates time to every appointment because we believe thorough, unhurried care is the standard your pet deserves.

Meet the Doctors

The hands behind
four decades of care

Dr. Sharon Christie

Owner / Managing Veterinarian

Dr. Christie has been practicing veterinary medicine for over 35 years. A Purdue graduate and a pillar in the Harrison County community, she is also on the Harrison County Board of Health and operates the annual rabies clinics each year.

Dr. Marie Gagnon

Surgeon / Associate Veterinarian

Dr. Gagnon joined Smith Animal Clinic in the spring of 2023 after graduating with her doctorate in 1993. In addition to clinic work, she also volunteers at spay/neuter clinics in the USA and abroad.
