Our Practice

Built around the work ranchers actually need

Like many practicing veterinarians, Dr. Kevin Dickey set out to build a clinic that could do both, the small-animal medicine families rely on and the large-animal work that keeps a ranch running.

That mixed focus is rare, and it is the reason neighbors call the same number for a kitten and a calf.
