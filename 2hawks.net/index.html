The Story

FROM THE
MISSOURI RIVER
VALLEY

Devin Price was 10 years old when he made his first knife. By 13 he had purchased the 2Hawks tomahawk business from Bob Thalmann in Fort Benton, Montana, and began carrying on a legacy that dates back to an 1884 cutler's shop on the upper Missouri River.

Today, from his Missouri farm, Devin is one of only a handful of high-quality tomahawk makers in the United States. His hawks have flown to Scotland, Germany, Norway, Iraq, and beyond.
