The Story

A Lifetime of Craft on the Water

Henry A. "Mac" McCarthy spent decades refining the art of lightweight cedar strip construction. His Wee Lassie design has become a benchmark for solo canoe builders around the world.

Feather Canoes carries that legacy forward today, with the same hand-drawn patterns and the same commitment to craftsmanship that started in 1984.
