Since day one

A hometown clinic that treats your animals like family

We opened our doors because this community needed a vet who would show up at midnight when a horse colics and remember your dog's name at the annual checkup. That has not changed.

Our practice covers small animals, large animals, and everything in between. We do not refer out what we can treat here, and we do not rush an exam because the waiting room is full.

Same vet, same staff, same phone number. The clinic has grown, but the way we practice has not.
