The fly shop

Red Deer's one stop fly shop

The shop sits at 3829-50A Street in Red Deer, serving Central Alberta and beyond with fly tying and fly fishing needs. Anglers call it the one stop shop, and the reason is simple: the same person guiding your trip ties the flies and teaches the cast.

Walk in for a custom fly, book a lesson, or plan a day on the river. It all happens under one roof.

Our story

Built for the angler who would rather be in the river

StreamWeaverFlies started as a way to share a lifetime on Central Alberta's rivers. Today it is a working fly shop where the guidance, the flies, and the teaching all come from the same bench. No distractions, no filler, just the next good day on the water.
