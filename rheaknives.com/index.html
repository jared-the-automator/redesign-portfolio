The Workshop

Where Steel Becomes a Tool

Our shop in Prattsville, Arkansas is where the work happens. There is no conveyor belt, no overseas assembly, no mass production. Just a forge, grinders, a workbench, and the experience that comes from years of forging blades.

Rhea has earned a Mastersmith rating from the American Bladesmith Society, a recognition held by a small number of smiths worldwide. That expertise shows in every knife that leaves the workshop.

Whether you need a field blade for hunting, a Bowie for the collection, or a custom piece you have sketched out, the process starts with a conversation and ends with a knife made to work.
