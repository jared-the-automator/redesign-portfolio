A legacy of care in our community

Scatter Creek Animal Clinic was founded by Dr. Randy Thomas and Carol Thomas in 1978. Over the decades, the practice has evolved to meet the changing needs of Rochester, Grand Mound, and surrounding communities in southwest Washington.

Today we see canine and feline patients, offering a full roster of general practice and preventive services. Our mission is simple: to keep your pets as healthy and pain free as possible for as long as possible.
