The Makers

27 Years at the Bench in the Inland Northwest

Joe and Charlene Douglas opened Black Dog Custom Rods in 1997, long before factory rods dominated the market. They believed then, as they do now, that a roditelier should build a rod the same way a luthier builds a guitar: by hand, with care, for a specific purpose.

Located in Otis Orchards, Washington, just east of Spokane, they have built rods for weekend anglers, professional tournament fishers, and commercial clients who sell private-label rods under their own brand.
