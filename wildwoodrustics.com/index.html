Our Process

From Log to Living Room

It starts in the timber yard. We select logs with character, not perfection. In our Mifflinburg workshop, those logs become beds, tables, and cabinets that carry the warmth of the forest into your home.

Each piece is sanded by hand and finished with care. The result is furniture that feels alive, with grain patterns and knots that tell the story of the tree it came from.
