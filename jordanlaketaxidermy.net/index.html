How a Piece Comes Together

01

Personal Attention

I do not sub out work to other taxidermists. Each piece stays in my hands from intake to finish.

02

Limited Volume

I limit the number of pieces I take in each year so nothing gets rushed and every mount gets the time it deserves.

03

Lifelike Details

Bringing out lifelike details is what separates a fair mount from a great one. I adjust daily while the hide dries. No pins, no clips.
