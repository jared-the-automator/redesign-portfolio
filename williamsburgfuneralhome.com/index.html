Our heritage

A Kingstree home since 1961.

Williamsburg Funeral Home was founded in March of 1961 by a group of community-minded neighbors who saw a need and built a place families could turn to. Today Billy and "B" Horton carry that same promise forward as a family-operated home.

For more than sixty years we have served Kingstree and the towns of Williamsburg County, including Lane, Salters, Greeleyville, Trio, and Cades.
