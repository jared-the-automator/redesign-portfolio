Each bird begins as a sketch on rough-cut basswood, shaped by hand with chisels and power tools.

The Craft

Seven Steps
to Perfection

From a rough sketch on basswood to the final mounted piece, each stage of the process is done by hand in the Birdhug Studio workshop in Rosendale, New York.

The carving process takes patience, skill, and an intimate knowledge of bird anatomy. Each feather is burned in with a pyrography pen. Every color is hand-mixed and applied in layers until the bird almost seems ready to take flight.
