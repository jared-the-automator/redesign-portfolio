About Arthur

A conservationist who writes from the prairie

Arthur Melville Pearson wrote the biography of George Fell, founder of the Natural Land Institute, and spends his time between the tallgrass at Midewin and the conservation lands of Jo Daviess County. His talks and walks grow out of that daily, close work with the land.

The result is a practice that pairs field expertise with storytelling, whether he is leading a group to a migrating warbler or speaking to a full room about prairie history.
