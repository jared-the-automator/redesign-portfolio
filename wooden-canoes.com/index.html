The Work

Built to Outlast
Its Owner

Rollin Thurlow co-authored "The Wood and Canvas Canoe" in 1988, the first book to fully document traditional construction techniques. It has not been out of print since.

Every restoration starts with a complete teardown. Each rib, plank, and fastening is assessed. A properly restored boat comes back rated for 75% of all canoeing demands and another lifetime of service.

The Shop

One Craftsman, One Shop, Central Maine

Rollin Thurlow runs the shop in Atkinson, Maine, with a small part-time staff. Every canoe that leaves is custom-built. There is no production line.

Classes and kits are also available for builders who want to construct their own. Rollin has taught at the Wooden Boat School and written the definitive reference on the craft.
