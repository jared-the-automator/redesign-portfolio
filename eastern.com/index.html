Our story

A hardware store, a tank of gas, and 90 years.

In 1932 the Clement family ran a busy hardware store in Danvers, Massachusetts, and started selling bottled gas for home and outdoor use. Demand grew until 1959, when they expanded under the name Country Gas Distributors.

Today Eastern is one of the largest family-owned energy providers in the Northeast, still run by the same family, still answering the phone.
