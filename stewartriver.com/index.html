The Workshop

Learn the Craft

Join Alex Comb in the workshop for a hands-on class over a few weekends. Work with cedar strips, fiberglass cloth, and epoxy to shape a boat from raw materials.

Leave with a canoe or kayak you built yourself, ready for portage and paddle. No prior woodworking experience required. Just patience and a willingness to learn.
