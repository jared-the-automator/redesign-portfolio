The Zoller Story

Three Generations on the River

Levi Zoller was born and raised in Klickitat County. His grandfather Phil and father Tracy guided before him. When you fish with Levi, you're not just hiring a guide. You're hiring a family born and raised on this water.

Levi spends his off time expanding access to premium hunting locations and creating new ways to provide a successful fishing experience. The river is not a backdrop for his work. It is the work.
