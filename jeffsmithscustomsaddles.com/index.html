The Maker

Riders First. Saddles Follow.

Jeff started building saddles because he could not find one that fit his horse. Years later, each saddle still starts with the same question: What does this rider need?

Today, Jeff Smith's Custom Saddles are ridden in rodeos, ranches, and show arenas across the country. Each piece is built one at a time, by hand, in Texas.
