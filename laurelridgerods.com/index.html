A rod for the water you know

Having always been a fisherman and someone heavily involved in the outdoors, the builder started Laurel Ridge Rods to deliver a quality performance crafted species specific fishing tool to the hands of customers.

Chasing trout in Western Pennsylvania since childhood and building rods since college in the early 2000s, the focus has always been on understanding exactly how rods behave in certain conditions and projecting those ideas onto the bench.

Every rod is a collaboration between builder and angler, shaped by the species, the water, and the way you like to fish.
