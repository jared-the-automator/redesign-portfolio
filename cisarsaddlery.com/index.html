BUILT FOR THE HORSE AND RIDER

Every Cisar saddle begins with the horse. All-around, barrel race, cutting, roper, show, donkey, mule, and pack saddles each shaped to the tree and covered in vegetable-tanned leather.

Lay the Leather

The Craft

FROM HIDE TO HORSE

Karel Císař learned saddle making under masters who insisted on hand-worked leather and exact fit. No two horses are identical, so no two saddles should be either. Each tree is carved, each skirt shaped, each fender stitched by hand in the Czech Republic.

Vegetable tanning produces leather that breathes, softens, and darkens with use. A well-built saddle with a hand-shaped tree can serve a horse and rider for twenty years or more.
