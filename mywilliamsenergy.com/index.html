Our Story

Four generations.
One family. One promise.

Williams Energy started with a single truck and a handshake in 1919. Four generations later, we still answer the phone ourselves. We still know our customers by name. And we still show up — day or night, holiday or blizzard — because that is what a family business does.

Today we serve thousands of homes across the South Shore from our locations in Braintree and Marshfield. Heating oil, propane, HVAC, plumbing, and electrical — all under one roof, all backed by 24/7 emergency service.
