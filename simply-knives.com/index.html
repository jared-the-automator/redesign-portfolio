THE MAKER'S
STORY

Andreas Behringer works in a small workshop in Erkrath, Germany. He started knife making out of a creative urge to hold something in his hands. Before knives, he built wooden cabins, crafted motorcycle parts from carbon fiber, and wrote books.

Every knife he makes is unique and a part of him. He never rushes because he fears to lose the love for what he is doing. His goal is to craft something that lasts, something special and valuable enough for a father to pass to his sons.

A piece of art with a practical use. A companion that accompanies you on your adventures.
