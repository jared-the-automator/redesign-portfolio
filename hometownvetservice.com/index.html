Built small, on purpose

A mixed-animal practice works best when the same people see your animals year after year. Our team handles the everyday and the urgent, from a puppy's first exam to a herd health plan, without the rush of a big box clinic.

That is the whole idea behind the name. In a small town, your vet is a neighbor, and your animals are part of the family.

Our Story

A new clinic with a hometown idea

Hometown Veterinary Service opened its doors in April 2025, owned and operated by Dr. Sydney Hennenfent, a 2022 graduate of the Kansas State University College of Veterinary Medicine.
