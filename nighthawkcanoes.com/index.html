Handmade One at a Time.

Nighthawk Canoes was established in 2009 in Saint Paul, Minnesota, and is now located near Madison, Wisconsin. Benjamin Skroch, owner and builder, handcrafts every canoe with a focus on lightweight Kevlar construction, durability, and performance.

Since each canoe is built to order, your Nighthawk is literally one of a kind. We offer a large catalog of optional finishes and features found nowhere else, tailored to your paddling style.
