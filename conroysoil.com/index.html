Our Story

A Family Commitment to Southern Maine

Conroy's Oil Service is a third-generation oil company that has been providing home heating fuels for Southern Maine since 1942. Originally of Scarborough, Maine, we relocated to 897 Portland Road (Route 1) in Saco in November of 2016.

With our fleet of oil trucks we have the capacity to handle extreme winter volumes, but we also pride ourselves on providing our customers personal attention to their heating fuel needs and usage. We take the extra time with our customers that others do not in order to completely understand and cater to specific fuel needs.
