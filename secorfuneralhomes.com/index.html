Since 1928

The only locally owned family funeral home in Willard

E. Raymond and Lillian Secor opened the first location in 1928. Their son Tom joined the business in the 1950s, and the New Washington home was added around the same time. The Plymouth location came in 1973.

Gus Secor, Raymond and Lillian's grandson, has been directing since 1983. He's worked four decades of Huron County families through some of the hardest days of their lives. His daughter Madie completed her internship in 2022.

While corporate chains have acquired much of the funeral industry across Ohio, Secor has stayed independent, family-run, and local. That matters to the families who call at 2 a.m.
