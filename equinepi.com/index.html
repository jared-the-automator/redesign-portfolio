What we do

Care that reads lameness where it starts.

  • 01

    Advanced Diagnostic Imaging

    Standing MRI and digital radiography that show the source of a performance problem, not just the symptom.

  • 02

    Lameness & Sports Medicine Exams

    Gait analysis and targeted workups built for the demands of the working and competition horse.

  • 03

    Regenerative Therapy

    Stem cell and PRP treatments that shorten recovery and help horses return to the work they are bred for.

Our practice

A referral clinic and imaging center in one place.

Northwest Equine Performance is an equine sports medicine clinic and advanced diagnostic imaging center in Mulino, Oregon. Horses come here when a general workup has run its course and an owner needs an answer.

The team works alongside referring veterinarians across the region, pairing hands-on lameness expertise with imaging that most practices cannot offer on site.
