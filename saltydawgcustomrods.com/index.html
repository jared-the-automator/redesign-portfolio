The Story

From Sign Paint to Rod Wraps

Bill's step-grandfather first taught him how to hand-wrap rods in the late 60s, mostly bamboo rods on a notched cardboard box and a phone book for a thread tensioner. He would do his own repairs and build an occasional rod over the years.

After working as a sign painter for two master sign crafters, then as a cabinet finisher for 25 years, Bill returned to rod building in 2004 when he saw pictures of beautiful custom rods on BD Outdoors.

He brings a little bit from all his sign painting and cabinet finishing knowledge and incorporates it into the design and building of custom fishing rods. So there ya have it. Give me a call and order your custom rod today.
