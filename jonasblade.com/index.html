The Craft

Fire, Steel, and Patience

Zack Jonas is a master bladesmith who has spent over a decade refining the art of hand-forging. Each blade starts as raw steel and ends as a tool that fits the hand like it was made for it, because it was.

No two knives leave the forge identical. The process demands heat shaping, hand grinding, and finish work that machines simply cannot replicate. That is what you are paying for.
