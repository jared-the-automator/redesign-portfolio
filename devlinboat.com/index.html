The Designer

Sam Devlin has spent his life on the water

From his father's boat shop to Alaskan fishing tugs and back to Olympia, Sam's love for wooden boats has shaped every design that leaves the shop. In 2012, Wooden Boat Magazine awarded him the Lifetime Achievement in Design.

His impact on the world of wooden boat building is second to none. Hundreds of boats completed at the shop now grace waters throughout the US and Canada, and builders in over 85 countries have turned his plans into seaworthy realities.
