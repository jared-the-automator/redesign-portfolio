Our Story

Forty-seven years of canvas tradition

Red Hawk Trading began in 1978 with a single sewing machine and a belief that outdoor shelters should be built to last, not replaced every season. Today, four decades later, we still cut every panel by hand and inspect every stitch before it leaves our workshop.

Our canvas wall tents and tipis have sheltered hunters in Montana, guided expeditions in Alaska, and served as base camp for families exploring the American West. The same principles guide us now as they did then: quality materials, honest construction, and a tent you can pass down.
