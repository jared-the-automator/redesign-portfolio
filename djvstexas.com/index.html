What Dr. Boeche does

Full equine care, delivered to the barn

One truck, one veterinarian, and the equipment to handle most of what your horse needs without a trailer ride. Care happens where your horse is calm: at home.

01

Mobile medicine and dentistry

Wellness exams, vaccinations, Coggins, and full floats done in your own barn aisle.

02

Acupuncture and chiropractic

As a Certified Veterinary Acupuncturist, Dr. Boeche treats horses, dogs, and cats with acupuncture, chiropractic, and Chinese medicine.

03

Lameness and herbal therapy

Practical workups for the performance horse, paired with herbal options when they fit the case.

04

In-home euthanasia

When it is time, a gentle goodbye at home, on familiar ground, without a final trailer ride.

Meet the vet

Dr. Jenn Boeche, DVM, CVA

Jenn grew up in northern Nevada showing Ponies of the Americas from the local level all the way to nationals, so she knew horses long before she was a doctor.

She earned bachelor's degrees in Animal Science and Spanish from Colorado State University, then her doctorate in veterinary medicine, and added certification in veterinary acupuncture.

Today she runs Diamond J as a solo mobile practice, and raises working stock dogs on the side.

How a barn call works

No waiting room, no trailer, no stress on the horse. Three steps from your first message to a treated horse.

Step one

Reach out

Send an email or request an appointment with your horse's issue and your location in North-Central Texas.

Step two

She comes to you

Dr. Boeche arrives with a fully stocked mobile rig and works your horse in its own barn or paddock.

Step three

Plan and follow up

You leave with a clear treatment plan and a direct line back to the vet who saw your horse.
