THE CAPTAIN

From Law Enforcement
to Legendary Angler

Capt. Chad Starling spent 24 years as a lieutenant with the Jacksonville Sheriff's Office. In 2006, he traded chasing criminals for chasing fish. The name Team Buck Rogers honors his late grandfather "Buck" and his father "Roger," the men who taught him to fish.

What started as one boat has grown into one of the most trusted charter operations on the Northeast Florida coast. Voted number one in Northeast Florida in 2019, 2020, and 2021. Professional, family-friendly, and child-safe.
