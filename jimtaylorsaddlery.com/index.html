50 Years in the Saddle

Jim Taylor has spent most of his life in the company of horses. He has trained, ridden, and shown in everything from western pleasure to reining and working cowhorse.

In 1971, he purchased a western saddle store in the Pacific Northwest. After building his craft for a decade, he left for Texas in 1981 to start a new venture in saddle making.
