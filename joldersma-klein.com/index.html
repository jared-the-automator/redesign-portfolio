Started by Anna Joldersma, one of Michigan's first women to lead a funeral home

Edward and Anna Joldersma opened the doors in 1913 at 923 East Main Street. In 1917 Anna earned her mortuary science license and became one of Michigan's first female funeral directors.

The home moved to 917 S. Burdick Street in 1950. Today brothers Dan and Gabe Adams carry the same promise their great-grandparents began: serve families as you would want yours served.
