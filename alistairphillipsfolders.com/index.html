The Maker

From weekend course to Spyderco collaboration.

My interest in knife making began in 2004 after attending a course taught by Karim Haddad at the Tharwa Valley Forge in Canberra. I had always had an interest in folding knives and very quickly I began to specialize in the craft of making a folding knife.

A full member of the Australian Knife Makers Guild, one of my proudest moments was winning the award for the Best Folding Knife at the 2012 Australian Knife Makers Guild show with a stag-handled three-bladed stockman folder.

Credibility

One of the most anticipated Spyderco knives of 2018, the Kapara is my first collaboration with Spyderco.

The design is based on my Redback folder. Its CPM S30V stainless steel blade is full-flat ground and has a fully accessible Trademark Round Hole for ambidextrous one-handed opening.
