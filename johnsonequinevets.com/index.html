Our Story

From a mixed animal practice to a dedicated equine clinic.

The Johnson family has been serving the North Lewisburg community and surrounding Ohio horse owners for years. What began as a mixed animal practice evolved into a focused equine veterinary clinic because we saw how much the horse community needed dedicated, knowledgeable care.

Today, Johnson Veterinary Clinic is a trusted partner for horse owners across Ohio. We combine decades of veterinary experience with a genuine passion for equine health.
