Day Permits

Fish by the day. No commitment.

Bank fishing, fly only, from 8am to one hour after dusk. Full day permits are £20. Evening permits £15 (May to August).

01

Full Day

£20. Arrive at 8am. Catch limit of three fish over 8 inches. Text your catch return to 07851 011085.

02

Evening Permit

£15. Valid 4pm to 11pm or dusk. Two fish limit. Available 1st May to 31st August.

03

Under 16s Fish Free

Up to two children per paying adult, sharing the adult catch limit. No separate permit needed.

The Location

A natural brown trout fishery at the foot of the Cauldstane Slap.

Harperrigg is a large reservoir approximately 10 miles west of Edinburgh. Situated south of the A70, west of Balerno, it is one of the few natural brown trout only fisheries in the area. Challenging and easily accessible, minutes away from the capital.
