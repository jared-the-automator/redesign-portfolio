A mixed-animal practice since 1967.

Dr. Robert Marston and Dr. Thomas Candee founded Amesbury Animal Hospital in 1967 to serve both the family pets and the working animals of the Merrimack Valley.

Dr. Kirk Smith joined in 1991 and carries that founding idea forward: one hospital, equally at home with a Labrador and a herd. Nearly six decades later, clients still return because it feels like family.
