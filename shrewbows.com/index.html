The Mountain Man Behind the Bow

Ron LaClair was born in 1936. At six years old he took his first bow and started hunting along the creek behind his parents' house.

His first rabbit in 1959 was only the beginning. Today Ron still hunts, still tests every bow he builds, and still believes a handmade wooden bow is the only way to take game with honor.

"By God, I are a mountain man, and I'll live 'til an arrow or a bullet finds me."
