The First Licensed Llama Outfitter in Yellowstone

Started in 1984 as the first licensed llama outfitting company in Yellowstone National Park, Yellowstone Llamas continues its vision and commitment to provide enjoyable, safe, and unforgettable experiences in the outdoors with llamas.

What began as a single trail and a couple of llamas has grown into one of the most respected outfitting experiences in the northern Rockies. Our guides have decades of combined experience, and our animals are trained, gentle, and ready for the trail.
