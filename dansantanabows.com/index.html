01 / CRAFT

Every Bow Starts
as a Tree

I select each stave by hand, looking for straight grain and tight rings. Osage, hickory, maple - each species has its own character and its own voice when it speaks through the string.

A bow is not just a tool. It is a conversation between the bowyer, the wood, and the archer who will draw it.

01

Select the Stave

Grain, density, and species determine the bow's soul.

02

Shape by Hand

Drawknife and scraper remove everything that is not the bow.

03

Tiller to Target

Each bow is matched to the archer's draw length and strength.

02 / LEARN

Bow Making
Classes

Learn to make a bow from a single board. My build-along videos and in-person classes take you from raw lumber to finished bow over a weekend. No power tools, no shortcuts - just you, the wood, and the patience to listen to what it wants to become.

Whether you are new to bowyers or have been making bows for years, there is always more to learn from the wood. Join the r/Bowyer community and share your progress.
