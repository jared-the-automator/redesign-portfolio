Bitten by the Bug in the Mid-Sixties

"When I made my first pair of leather moccasins at a Boy Scout camp in the mid sixties, I was hooked. The tongues of old boots became knife sheaths, slingshot pouches, and other items of dubious use."

In the late nineties, Gene developed an interest in Cowboy Action Shooting. Disappointed with the available holsters and belts, he got his tools out, blew off the dust, and made his own gear. Friends and fellow shooters began asking for more.

Gene Weishuhn, Custom Leathersmith and Owner
