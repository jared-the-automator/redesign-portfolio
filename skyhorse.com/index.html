The Craft

Built to the measurement.
Carved to the story.

01

Handmade wooden trees

Every tree is built to your horse's exact measurements: specific bar width, fork shape, horn style, and cantle dimensions. Not a catalog tree. Built for your horse.

02

Lisa's hand-carved wildlife imagery

Every design is drawn, sculpted, and painted directly into the leather by Lisa. Eagles, bears, wildlife: no transfers, no templates. Each saddle is singular.

03

Loren's signature edge braiding

Eight-strand kangaroo leather lace or pure silver: Loren's braiding technique is a Skyhorse trademark unique in the industry. Fork, cantle, horn, any edge.

We start with your horse, not a template.

Every saddle begins with your horse's measurements and your vision. Fit is built before any carving starts. Structure before art. Every Skyhorse saddle ships ready to ride: cinch, stirrups, and saddle strings included.

Base price: $8,500

$2,500 deposit locks your delivery date and design. Custom tooling quoted individually. Price is firm once your design is approved.
