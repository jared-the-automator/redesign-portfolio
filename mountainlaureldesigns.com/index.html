Our Story

Built by hand
in Virginia since 2002

Mountain Laurel Designs was started by Ron Bell in 2002. His lightweight background spans over 40 years, from Search and Rescue in Yosemite to Ironman triathlons.

After you sleep inside a haul bag on a big wall, you realize how little you need to reach your goals. That philosophy drives every piece of gear we build.

Our products are proudly handmade in Roanoke, Virginia and shipped worldwide to outdoor lovers everywhere.

Philosophy

Simplicity.
Self-Reliance.
Adventure.

Selecting a minimal number of SUL multiple-use gear, and only what we really need, creates the opportunity to transcend daily life and discover more. It is not about suffering or denial. It is about purity of form.

Traveling longer trail distances efficiently and via ecologically sound principles creates a challenge and forms a basis for a new way of experiencing life.
