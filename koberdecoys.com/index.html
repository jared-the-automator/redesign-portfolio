The Story Behind the Decoys

A Name Museums and Collectors Trust

In 1990 Dave Kober received the Michigan Heritage Award, the state's highest honor for traditional craft. His decoys hang in the Michigan State University Museum and the Bass Pro Shop in Springfield, Missouri.

Kober decoys live in the private collections of Mort Neff of Michigan Outdoors, HGTV's Pete Nelson, MSU Basketball's Tom Izzo, and world-renowned carver Jim Foote.

Every decoy is still painted by hand in the same Northern Michigan workshop where Grandpa Ballard taught the trade.
