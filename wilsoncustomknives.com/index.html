The Maker

Sam Wilson started
Wilson Custom Knives
in 2014.

He set out to build the knives he wanted to use: blades that could survive a season of hard work and still look like art. Ten years later, he is still doing it the same way. One at a time. By hand. In Oregon.

Every Wilson knife ships with a lifetime warranty and the promise that if you break it, Sam will fix it. That is the kind of accountability you only get from the person whose name is on the blade.
