Built by Hand

32 Years of Flintlock Work in Virginia

Troy Roope was born and raised in Stonewall, Virginia. He started building rifles in 1994 and has competed with them since. He works with traditional hand tools and machines only the parts that require it.

His mentors include Jim Hash of Appomattox, Virginia and the gunsmiths at Colonial Williamsburg. If you need a custom flintlock or percussion gun, call him.

The Dickert Style Rifle

42-inch Colerain barrel in .50 caliber. Siler Flintlock. RE Davis double-set trigger. Curly maple stock with wooden patchbox. Cash thimbles with octagon profile and wedding bands. Silver front sight. Investment-cast butt plate, trigger guard, and side plate. Tenons pinned with 1/16 inch pins.
