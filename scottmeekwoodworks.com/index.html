An Intimacy with the Wood
You Can't Get Any Other Way

Scott started as a trim carpenter, moved into custom cabinetry, and got hooked the first time he took a full-width ribbon shaving off a properly tuned hand plane. He went all in.

"Creating something with hand tools forces me to focus more intently on what I am doing - how I use the grain of the wood, what type of joinery I will use. Each piece is created with passion, focus, and a desire to learn something new."
