A Garage.
A Bow That
Didn't Exist.

Rick Ellis wanted a small, sleek hunting bow that no one made. So he built one from his garage in Ohio. Those first prototypes turned into Striker Bows.

Twenty-nine years later, Rick's sons Dusty and John work the shop alongside him. Every bow still ships from Ohio. Every bow is still built by hand.

"They have different stories but one common theme: they love their bow."
