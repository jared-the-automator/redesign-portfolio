31 Years in Midland

The practice Midland has trusted since before the millennium.

Dr. Riordan Hartzoge established Town & Country Veterinary Hospital in 1995 after completing his Doctorate of Veterinary Medicine at Texas A&M. He built a practice that handles routine care and advanced surgical cases in the same building, so your dog never gets referred across town.

His daughter, Dr. Nicole Hartzoge Janik, joined the practice in 2022. Three licensed doctors. One location. Orthopedic surgery, ultrasonography, and the day-to-day care that keeps a family's pets healthy for years.
