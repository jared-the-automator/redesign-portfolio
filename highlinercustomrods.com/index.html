How every rod is built

No two rods leave this shop the same.

01

You choose every detail

Thread colors, grip shape, blank, components. We work through it together by phone or email until the specs are exactly what you want on the water.

02

Finest materials only

CF Burkheimer, RL Winston, Sage, Orvis, and Talon blanks. Cork from Portugal, Superior Grade. No substitutions, no compromises on components.

03

Numbered, named, shipped to you

Photos of your finished rod arrive before your final payment. Your name and specs go on before it ships, in a cloth bag and custom aluminum case.

Built to cast farther than the factory blank it came from.

Through a guide train modification developed over 40 years, every Highliner rod out-casts the factory version of the same blank. The exact distance added depends on the angler. The improvement is consistent.

I fish these rivers myself. The Deschutes, the McKenzie, the Columbia. A spey rod for salmon and steelhead work on Oregon water is built from that experience, not from a catalog spec sheet.

Highliner Custom Rods, Bend Oregon

Highliner Custom Rods is a one-person shop. Over 40 years of custom rod building, starting as a hobby and becoming a business. Every rod is built by one pair of hands.

25 years in the Pacific Northwest. Familiar with the Deschutes, the McKenzie, the Columbia, and the many fisheries those rivers hold. When not building, I'm fishing.
