The Rod Maker

Ron Barch Builds Each Rod by Hand

Ron Barch has been working with fishing rods for more than 25 years, first assembling fiberglass and graphite blanks, and eventually graduating to making split bamboo fly rods. He enjoys a challenge and believes split cane fly rods should be unique creations and personal expressions of the craftsman.

01

Split Cane Construction

Each rod is built from carefully selected bamboo, split and planed to precise tapers that enhance the angling experience.

02

Custom Tapers

Ron uses his experience as an angler to select tapers and methods of construction for rods that function exceptionally well.

03

Hand Varnished

Every rod receives multiple coats of hand-applied varnish for a finish that protects and endures.

The Library

Books on Split Cane

Alder Creek Angling is a purveyor of fine custom-crafted bamboo fly rods and a publisher of books related to the lore of split cane rods.

Ron has used his experience as an angler to assist in selecting tapers and methods of construction. As a custom rod maker, he is always pleased to work with clients in an attempt to provide that "Perfect Rod" for your specific needs.
