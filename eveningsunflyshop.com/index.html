Guided Trips

Fish 50+ Rivers with a Guide Who Knows Them All

Charlie is intimately familiar with over 50 area rivers and streams. From the Swift to the Farmington and Deerfield, he knows where the fish are holding and which flies to throw.

Guided trips cover dry fly, nymphing and streamer techniques. You will leave confident and ready to fish on your own.

The Shop

A Place Built by Anglers, for Anglers

Evening Sun Fly Shop is more than a retail space. It is a classroom, a meeting place, and a starting point for adventures on rivers across New England and beyond. Charlie served for two years as president of the Squan-a-tissit Chapter of Trout Unlimited. That community focus still runs through everything the shop does.
