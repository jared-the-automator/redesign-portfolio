About Charlie

Decades on the Water

Charlie Shadan has been fly fishing and fly tying for decades. He served two years as president of the Squan-a-tissit Chapter of Trout Unlimited. His ambition is simple: make fly fishing accessible to anyone who wants to learn.

He is intimately familiar with over 50 area rivers and streams and offers custom kits, fly collections, and training schedules for individuals, organizations, and municipalities.
