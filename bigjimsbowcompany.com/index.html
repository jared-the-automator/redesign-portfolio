Proud of Every One.

Big Jim Babcock has personally built hundreds of custom longbows and take down bows from his workshop in Albany, Georgia. He is proud of every one of them and confident that they are some of the best shooting bows on the market today.

From traditional archers looking for their first hand-built take down to seasoned hunters upgrading to a Mountain Monarch or Desert Bighorn Recurve, Big Jim builds each bow with the same care he put into his first.
