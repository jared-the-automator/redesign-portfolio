Since 1882

A family tradition
of care

In 1882, Henry Linnemann entered the funeral business with Edward Moore in Covington. Over six generations, the Linnemann family has served Northern Kentucky with unwavering compassion.

On July 4, 1955, Bud and Alice Linnemann opened the Erlanger funeral home in the historic mansion that still serves families today. In 2009, we built Northern Kentucky’s only on-site crematory.

In January 2017, Louis Linnemann purchased the business from his parents, making Linnemann Funeral Homes officially a 6th generation family business with over 140 years of continuous service.
